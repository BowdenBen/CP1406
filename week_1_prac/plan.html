Goal

Increase awareness of Training Zone among local residents (18–45), leading to at least a 40% increase in local customers.

The problem identified by the client is low visibility. Therefore the website must:

  • Clearly communicate who Training Zone is
  • Clearly explain what services are offered
  • Make the organisation visible and credible
  • Encourage local residents to consider training

Success Evaluation

  • Local customer numbers increase by at least 40% after launch.
  • Measurable increase in enquiries from local residents.
  • Increased traffic to course pages.

Target Audience

  • Local residents of Cairnsville
  • Aged 18–45
  • Mainly mature-age job seekers
