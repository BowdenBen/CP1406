Goal

The goal of the Vcare Opticals website is to increase the volume of appointment enquiries and service bookings by streamlining access to clinic information and special offers. Specifically, the site aims to improve the conversion of local web traffic into email-based leads by highlighting the "20% email booking discount" and the "Annual Eye Checkup" package. This mission provides a measurable benefit to the client by shifting customer intent from general browsing to direct contact.

Success Evaluation

The site's success will be evaluated through the following quantifiable metrics:

  • Booking Volume: Counting the number of incoming emails containing the pre-set subject line "Eye Exam Booking (from website)" generated by the site's mailto links.
  • Promotion Uptake: Tracking the redemption of the 20% "first exam" discount and the Annual Eye Checkup package specifically mentioned on the site.
  • Efficiency: Applying the "Two-Click Rule"—testing that a user can reach a contact point or service list from any page on the site within two clicks.

These measures directly evaluate the mission by proving the website is generating new business leads rather than just receiving "hits."

Target Audience

The primary audience consists of local residents in Razole and surrounding suburbs. This includes:

  • Families and Parents: Looking for routine check-ups and reliable community care.
  • Seniors/Older Adults: Requiring specialized screenings (Glaucoma, Cataracts) and clear, readable health information.
  • Busy Professionals: Seeking quick access to trading hours and a fast way to book via mobile devices.

How the site will be designed for this audience:

  • Information Design: Content from the client email will be reformatted into scannable chunks, using bulleted lists for services and clear <h2> headings for eye health articles.
  • Accessibility: High-contrast typography and large, "tap-friendly" buttons will be used to ensure usability for seniors and mobile users.
  • Responsiveness: A mobile-first CSS approach with three distinct breakpoints will ensure the site is professional and consistent across phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Trust Building: Prominent placement of the clinic's 25-year history and staff credentials to establish immediate professional credibility.
